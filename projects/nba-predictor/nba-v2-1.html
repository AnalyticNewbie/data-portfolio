v2.1: The Sunday Fix

Debugging Timezones and "Rest Disadvantage."

The Anomaly

The model was performing well on Tue-Sat, but failing miserably on Sunday/Monday games. It turned out to be a Timezone Bug. Games played Sunday night in NY were being logged as Monday morning in my database, breaking the "Days of Rest" calculation. 

# The Fix: Forcing US/Eastern Time
dt_utc = datetime.strptime(game_date, "%Y-%m-%dT%H:%M:%S")
dt_et = dt_utc.astimezone(timezone('US/Eastern'))
# Now 'Rest Days' aligns with reality

The Impact

Once fixed, the model correctly identified teams on "Back-to-Backs" (playing 2 nights in a row). This immediately stabilized the volatility metrics and prepared the groundwork for the v2.2 "Physics" update.
