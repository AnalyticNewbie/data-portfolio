v2.1: The Sunday Fix

Correcting data ingestion errors and stabilizing volatility.

The Anomaly

After launching v2.0, I noticed a spike in prediction errors specifically on Mondays. The model was aggressively betting against teams playing on Sunday, assuming they were fatigued.

The Bug:

My timezone conversion logic was flawed. Games played Sunday Night in the US were being logged as Monday Morning in Australia, but the "Rest Days" calculation was off by 24 hours.

The Solution

I rewrote the timestamp handling to enforce US/Eastern time for all game logic, regardless of where the script is run. I also introduced a specific feature for "Rest Disadvantage." 

# v2.1 Logic Update
def calculate_rest(team_id, game_date):
    prev_game = get_last_game(team_id, game_date)
    # Forced timezone conversion to handle Sunday night games
    delta = (game_date - prev_game).days
    return delta if delta < 4 else 4  # Cap rest at 4 days
