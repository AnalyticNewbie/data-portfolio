1. The Monday Anomaly

While auditing model failures (v2.1), I discovered a massive correlation between high error rates and the day of the week.

An investigation revealed that Home Court Advantage disappears on Mondays. Whether due to "Sunday Travel Hangover" or quiet crowds, the win percentage drops significantly.

The Data Proof:

  • Tuesday Home Win %: 66.7% (Dominant)
  • Sunday Home Win %: 57.1% (Standard)
  • Monday Home Win %: 51.6% (Coin Flip)
