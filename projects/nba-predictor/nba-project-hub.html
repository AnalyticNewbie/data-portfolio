NBA Predictor v4.1

An automated end-to-end Machine Learning pipeline that ingests daily NBA data, models team physics (Pace/Rebounding), and identifies value edge against Vegas betting lines.

The Hypothesis

Most public betting models rely on simple "Points Per Game" averages. My hypothesis is that by modeling the "texture" of a game—how specific matchups (e.g., Fast Pace vs. Elite Transition Defense) interact—we can predict outcomes with higher accuracy than the market consensus.

This project is a self-correcting system. It runs daily, bets "virtually," tracks its own errors, and allows me to iterate on the features (Engineering) rather than the prediction (Guessing).
